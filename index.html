Deerhunter
“Halcyon Digest”

This site is a love letter for Deerhunter’s “Halcyon Digest,” an album with a shimmery, melancholic sound which evokes for me the urban-suburban landscape in which I grew up.

On this site, each song from the album has a symbol which I drew while listening to it. On the page for each song, its symbol becomes a lens through which the landscape of my childhood can be wandered.

Joseph Allegro, 2018
